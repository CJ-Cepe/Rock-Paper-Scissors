Instruction

  1. Choose one Pokeball per round.
  2. Each Pokeball corresponds to an element.
  3. To score, the element type you choose must beat the opponent's type:
    • Water beats Fire
    • Grass beats Water
    • Fire beats Grass
  4. The first to score 6 points wins the game.
